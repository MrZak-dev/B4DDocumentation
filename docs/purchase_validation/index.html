Purchase Validation

Validate your item and get a license key

In order to get the license key to run your game visit this LINK

1- Login using your Envato Account used to buy the Item

2- After login you should be able to get your license key

The Purchase code is delivered to you by envato The Package Name is the one you want to use with your game in the Export To Android Process.

3- After filling the forms with your item , package name and your purchase , you will se a license key generated at the bottom , click on the form and CTRL + A to select all , then Copy and keep it safe in a txt file .

you will need your license key to create your game .
